about

the poet

Satish Rohatgi writes poems the way a letter gets written — unhurried, addressed to someone in particular, even when that someone is a memory, a season, or a thought that wouldn't leave him alone.

This page is his corkboard: pinned drafts, taped-down lines, work gathered as it's written rather than all at once.

— S.R.
