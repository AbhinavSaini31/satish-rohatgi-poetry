Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Comments: if you leave a comment on a poem, we store the name you provide (or "Anonymous" if left blank) and the comment text.
  • Likes: if you like a poem, an anonymous counter is incremented; your browser locally remembers which poems you've liked so you can't like the same poem twice, but no personal identity is tied to this.
  • Automatically collected data: like most websites, our hosting and advertising providers may automatically log standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use partner sites or apps by visiting Google's Advertising Policies page.

Third-Party Services

Your Choices

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features (like remembering which poems you've liked) may not work as intended if you do. You may also opt out of interest-based advertising through the links above.
